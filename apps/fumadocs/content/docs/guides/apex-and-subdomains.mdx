---
title: Handle apex domains and subdomains
description: Respect adapter capabilities without guessing public-suffix boundaries.
---

Domain SDK validates full hostnames but does not guess registrable apex domains with a two-label rule and does not bundle a public-suffix database. Use `domains.capabilities.apexDomains` for provider support.

Railway apex instructions use ALIAS/ANAME or CNAME flattening. Vercel supports apex configuration. Cloudflare for SaaS v0.1 exposes CNAME routing and reports apex support unavailable. Wildcards are rejected everywhere in v0.1.
